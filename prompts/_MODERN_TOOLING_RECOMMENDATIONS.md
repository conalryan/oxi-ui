- runtime comparisson:
  - node.js
  - bun
  - deno
- bundler comparison
  - oxc
  - vite
  - esbuild
  - parcel2
- linting comparison
  - eslint
  - Oxlint
- formatting comparison
  - Oxfmt
  - prettier
  - biome
